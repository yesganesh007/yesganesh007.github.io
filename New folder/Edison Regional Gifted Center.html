Vision Statement of
EDISON RGC

At Edison Regional Gifted Center, we strive to meet the academic and social needs of our
diverse population of gifted and academically advanced students through an
integrated and authentic curriculum, differentiated instruction, the use of technology,
and partnership within the community.
